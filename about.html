We are fully approved by the Horse Society as a professional riding school

We ride on horses and ponies chosen for their safe temperament, catering for riders of all abilities. We offer training packages tailored to your needs and requirements, catering to all abilities. There is also the opportunity to join us at competitions both at home and abroad, getting the utmost enjoyment out of the sport as you progress in a fun and friendly atmosphere. Previous experience is not necessary.
