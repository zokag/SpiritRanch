About our academy

Our modern and fully equipped ranch is happy to offer everything from polo and show-jumping to horse riding trips across the hills and mountains. Energise your mind, body and soul with a visit to our spacious and accommodating center.

At our centre you will acquire new riding skills or simply will have a chance to relax in a good atmosphere. The program is taught by experienced staff and caters to all riding abilities, from beginners to advanced.

Bonnie

Bonnie, the light brown mare, exudes a gentle and enchanting presence. Her coat showcases a delicate and warm shade of light brown, reminiscent of caramel or toasted almonds. The coloration evokes a sense of softness and grace.


What truly catches the eye is Bonnie's magnificent golden mane. It cascades down her neck in waves of shimmering strands, glistening under the sunlight like molten gold. The radiant mane adds a touch of brilliance to her overall appearance, contrasting beautifully with her light brown coat.

Clyde

Clyde, the dark brown male horse, stands tall and looks powerful. His coat is a deep shade of brown, almost black.


On Clyde's forehead is a striking white stripe that extends from between his eyes, resembling a bold and distinctive strap. This mark adds a touch of uniqueness to Clyde's appearance, making him instantly recognizable.


With a strong and muscular physique, Clyde exudes strength and agility. His legs are well-defined, providing a solid foundation for his movements. His hooves, dark and sturdy, leave an impression with each purposeful step.

Shadow

Shadow, the black male horse, is made of mystery and strength. His sleek coat is as dark as night, exuding an aura of elegance and power.


With a muscular and powerful build, Shadow stands tall and proud. His physique is excelent. Each step he takes is so confident, you won't ever be scared with him.


His gaze carries a sense of intelligence and quiet determination, reflecting his strong personality.

Snow

Snow, the white mare, is a beauty. Her coat is resembling freshly fallen snow on a winter's day. The color white envelops her entire body, making her look so graceful.


With a graceful and delicate build, Snow moves with elegance. Her movements are light and fluid, as if she glides effortlessly across the ground.


Snow's eyes, likely a soft and gentle shade of brown or blue hold a calm and gentle gaze, revealing a depth of kindness in her. Her gaze is often filled with warmth, inviting a sense of peace and comfort.
