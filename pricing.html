horse riding prices

information

  • Nature rides are rides through forest with tuition if required.
  • Private Lesson are one-to-one with your own personal mounted escort.
  • Show Jumping Lesson is a class tailored to elevate your jumping skills and horse-rider harmony. Schedule these sessions at your convenience and let the experts guide you to reach your full potential.
  • Group Lesson can consist of a group of up to 8 riders with a shared escort. It's a fun way to meet new people!
  • The minimum age limit at the stables is 4 years.
  • Adult riders are 13 years of age and over.
  • The weight limit for riders is 180 lb / 80kg – please note our weight limits are strictly observed.
